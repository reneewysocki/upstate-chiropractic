Mission Statement

At Upstate Chiropractic and Wellness we are dedicated to getting our patients out of pain and back to doing what they love as quickly and safely as possible. Through the use of soft tissue techniques, chiropractic adjustments, and rehabilitative exercises, we are able to address the cause of our patient’s complaint, and prevent future re-injury.
