Manipulation/Mobilization

Chiropractic adjustments are used to restore motion to joints that have lost their full range of motion due to poor posture, trauma, or repetitive motions.

Active Release Techniques (ART)

A soft tissue technique which breaks up scar tissue that has formed in tight muscles while stretching the muscle, this improves muscle tone while also improving mobility.

Instrument Assisted Soft Tissue Massage

A metal tool is used to break up scar tissues and knots that have formed in muscles and other soft tissues, this encourages blood flow to the area as well as improved muscle tone.

Rehabilitation Exercises

Corrective exercises are used to address muscular imbalances and prevent re-injury.

Activator Technique

Another form of chiropractic manipulation that is more gentle than traditional chiropractic adjustments.

Postural Re-education

Exercises, stretches, and kinesiology taping are used to address poor posture often caused by cell phone usage or working at a desk job.

Prenatal and Postpartum Care

Chiropractic adjustments, rehab exercises, and stretching are used to improve a mother's alignment, strengthen the core and deal with pregnancy induced changes in weight and center of gravity.

RockTape Kinesiotaping

Kinesiology tape has many uses, such as improving injury healing time, postural re-education, decreasing swelling/edema, and supporting injuries.

Electric Muscle Stimulation

Electric stimulation blocks pain and helps with muscle spasm.

Therapeutic Ultrasound

A deep form of heat that helps to decrease inflammation and break up scar tissue.
